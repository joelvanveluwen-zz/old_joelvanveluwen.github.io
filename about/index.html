About me

I am currently based in Sydney, Australia working for the world's largest professional network - LinkedIn.

Prior to LinkedIn I was working at Quantium leveraging one of Australia's largest transaction sets to deliver the engine that drives Woolworths' loyalty personalisation efforts. I also got to work with some of the largest FMCG players in Australia.

Before starting my career in analytics I completed a dual degree program at The University of Queensland and was awarded degrees in Econommics and Commerce (Information Systems).

On the technology side - I'm a huge advocate for Apache Spark (a powerful cluster computing framework for analytics), Apache Zeppelin (an awesome data science notebook that can issue workflows to Spark) and D3.JS for data visualisation.
